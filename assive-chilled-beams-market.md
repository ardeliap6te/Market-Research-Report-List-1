<p><p>Passive chilled beams are used in HVAC systems for efficient cooling. The market can be categorized into two main types: bare type and mosaic type. Bare type chilled beams are simple, unobtrusive units that rely on building's air circulation to cool spaces, often embedded in ceilings. Mosaic type chilled beams, by contrast, are more decorative and integrated into architectural design, resembling ceiling tiles. This type allows for enhanced aesthetics while still providing effective thermal comfort and energy efficiency within various environments.</p></p>
