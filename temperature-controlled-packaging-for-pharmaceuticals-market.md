<p><strong>The Temperature Controlled Packaging for Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Temperature Controlled System</li><li>Passive Temperature Controlled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature Controlled Packaging for Pharmaceuticals is essential for ensuring the integrity of temperature-sensitive products during transportation and storage. Active Temperature Controlled Systems utilize external power sources, such as refrigeration units, to maintain specific temperature ranges, allowing for precise control throughout the supply chain. In contrast, Passive Temperature Controlled Systems rely on insulating materials and phase change materials to maintain temperature without active cooling or heating, providing a cost-effective solution for short-term temperature stability. Both systems are crucial for safeguarding pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
