<p><p>The Portable Multi Gas Detector market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Catalytic Combustion**: Detects flammable gases through heat generation from combustion.</p><p>2. **Electrochemical (echem)**: Utilizes chemical reactions to measure gas concentrations, ideal for toxic gases.</p><p>3. **Infrared (IR)**: Uses light absorption principles to identify gases based on their unique spectral fingerprints.</p><p>4. **Photoionization Detectors (PID)**: Employ ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, detecting volatile organic compounds effectively. </p><p>Each technology offers distinct advantages for specific applications in safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
