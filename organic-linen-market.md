<p><ul><li>Dry Spun Yarn</li><li>Wet Spun Yarn</li><li>Blended Yarn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic linen market comprises three primary types: dry spun yarn, wet spun yarn, and blended yarn. Dry spun yarn is produced by twisting fibers after drying, resulting in a crisp finish, ideal for lightweight textiles. Wet spun yarn involves spinning fibers in a moist environment, yielding a softer, more flexible texture, suitable for delicate fabrics. Blended yarn combines organic linen with other fibers, enhancing durability and versatility, making it popular for various applications in apparel and home textiles.</p></p>
