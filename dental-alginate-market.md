<p><p>The dental alginate market comprises several types, including sodium alginate, potassium alginate, and ammonium alginate. Sodium alginate is the most widely used in dental applications due to its excellent gelation properties and ease of use. Potassium alginate offers similar benefits but can enhance the strength and elasticity of impressions. Ammonium alginate is less common but serves specific applications in dental practices. Each type caters to various requirements in impression-making, ensuring accurate and reliable dental molds for diagnostic and treatment purposes.</p></p>
