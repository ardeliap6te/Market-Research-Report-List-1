<p><ul><li>H-Bridge Motor Driver Board</li><li>Dual H-Bridge Motor Driver Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor driver board market primarily includes H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge configurations. An H-Bridge motor driver board allows for bidirectional control of a single motor, efficiently enabling forward and reverse motion with PWM speed control. In contrast, a Dual H-Bridge motor driver board can control two motors independently, offering greater versatility for applications requiring multiple motor management, such as robotics and automation systems. Both types are essential in various sectors, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
