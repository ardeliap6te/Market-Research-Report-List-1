<p><p>The fitness and yoga mats market features various material types, each offering distinct benefits. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) mats are eco-friendly, lightweight, and provide good traction. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) mats offer excellent cushioning and durability, ideal for high-impact exercises. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) mats are soft, flexible, and resistant to wear. Natural rubber mats are biodegradable, providing superior grip and comfort. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) mats are affordable and widely used but less eco-friendly. Other materials include jute and microfiber, catering to diverse preferences.</p></p>
