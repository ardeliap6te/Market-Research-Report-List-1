<p><p>The outdoor pool heat pumps market comprises three main types: geothermal, air source, and water source heat pumps. Geothermal heat pumps utilize the stable temperatures underground, providing efficient heating and cooling. Air source heat pumps extract heat from the ambient air, making them suitable for moderate climates. Water source heat pumps draw heat from a nearby water source, offering efficient temperature regulation. Each type caters to different environmental conditions and user needs, enhancing energy efficiency and pool comfort year-round.</p></p>
