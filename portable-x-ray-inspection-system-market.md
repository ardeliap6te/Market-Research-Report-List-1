<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Inspection System market comprises two main types: FPD (Flat Panel Detector) X-Ray Inspection Systems and Film X-Ray Inspection Systems. FPD systems utilize digital detectors for real-time imaging, offering enhanced image quality and faster processing, making them ideal for immediate analysis. In contrast, Film X-Ray Inspection Systems rely on traditional film-based methods, which may be less efficient but are still widely employed in various applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, particularly in environments where digital technology is less accessible.</p></p>
