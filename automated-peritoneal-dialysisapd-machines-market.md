<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines are categorized into two main market types: power type and gravity type. Power type machines utilize electric pumps to efficiently regulate fluid movement and ensure precise dosing, enhancing treatment accuracy. In contrast, gravity type machines rely on gravity to facilitate fluid exchange, making them simpler and more portable. Both types serve patients with end-stage kidney disease, offering convenience and flexibility in managing dialysis at home while improving overall quality of life.</p></p>
