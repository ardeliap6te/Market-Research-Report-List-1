<p><strong>The 10-bit Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Native 10-bit Panel</li><li>8-bit Dithering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 10-bit monitor market features two main types: native 10-bit panels and 8-bit dithering. Native 10-bit panels use true hardware to display over a billion colors, providing superior color accuracy and gradient smoothness ideal for professional applications like graphic design and video editing. In contrast, 8-bit dithering simulates additional colors by blending pixel data, achieving a 10-bit effect. While more cost-effective, it can lead to artifacts and less precise color representation, making them suitable for casual use.</p></p>
