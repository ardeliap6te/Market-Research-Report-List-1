<p><p>The Airport Information Display System (AIDS) market is categorized into two main types: Terminal Side and Air Side. The Terminal Side Airport Information Display System manages real-time information for passengers within the terminal, including flight arrivals, departures, gate changes, and other essential notices. In contrast, the Air Side Airport Information Display System focuses on information relevant to flight operations, such as runway status, aircraft movements, and air traffic coordination, ensuring effective communication between ground and airborne operations.</p></p>
