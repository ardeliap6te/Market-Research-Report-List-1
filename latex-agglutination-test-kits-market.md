<p><strong>The Latex Agglutination Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA</li><li>Indirect Fuorescent Antibody Test</li><li>Hemagglutination Inhibition</li><li>Serum Neutralization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Latex agglutination test kits encompass various types, including ELISA, Indirect Fluorescent Antibody Test (IFAT), Hemagglutination Inhibition (HAI), and Serum Neutralization tests. ELISA detects antibodies or antigens, providing quantitative results. IFAT employs fluorescent-labeled antibodies for identifying specific antigens in samples. HAI measures the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus-induced agglutination of red blood cells, while Serum Neutralization assesses the effectiveness of an antibody against a virus. Each method serves distinct diagnostic and research purposes in immunology.</p></p>
